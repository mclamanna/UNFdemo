Mental Health Resources- Talk

Talk to a mental health professional for further support and resources.

Let's Talk

Consult with a UNFCC Counselor
The Let's Talk program provides easy access to informal, brief, virtual drop-in consultations with UNF counselors. Speaking with a counselor in a confidential setting can help you access support, provide perspective, help you explore resources and options and discuss how counseling might be helpful. Appointments are scheduled online and there is no paperwork to complete.
Individual Counseling

Book an appointment with a UNFCC counselor
If you are a current UNF student and are interested in seeking counseling services from the UNF Counseling Center, you may do so by walking into our main office (located in building 2) and scheduling with the front desk or by completing the appointment request form.
Psychiatry

Get a Referral to a UNFCC Psychiatric Provider
Students interested in an evaluation for psychiatric medication need first to meet with a counselor at the UNF Counseling Center.

For Long Term Therapy

Off Campus Referral Resources
Off Campus Referral Resources Therapists in the community can see students more frequently and longer term. If a student is seeking medication evaluation/management, they might also consider a community psychiatrist.

Distance Learning Students- Online Sessions

For students in enrolled in a distance learning program and who are located outside the state of Florida, Wellconnect is another option for ongoing therapy services through online video sessions.
