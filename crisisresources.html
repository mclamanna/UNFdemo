Crisis Resources

Urgent Drop-in Counseling For Students in Crisis:
Call (904) 620-2602
  • Students with urgent needs should come in for a walk-in appointment, Monday - Friday, 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM.
  • Or call the after-hours support line on 620-2602, select option 2.

Support following a Sexual Assault or Actual/Threatened Violence:
Call (904) 620-1010
  • Contact the Victim Advocacy Program for confidential, campus-based urgent support around sexual assault, sexual harassment, interpersonal violence, and stalking. The services offered range from emotional support to assistance navigating the criminal justice system.
  • 24/7 Crisis Intervention Line: (904) 620-1010.

Emergency Medical Care:
Please visit the Emergency and After-Hours Care services website provided by Student Health Services: Emergency Medical Care

Campus Tragedy/Campus Death:
Call (904) 620-2602
  • The Dean of Students (DOS) team serves as the UNF campus liaison for family, friends, and University officials when there is a student death.
  • UNF Counseling Center offers support to campus after student deaths and other traumatic events through our mental health providers. Our counselors can consult with you about the situation and can help assess what is needed.
  • For consultation please call (904) 620-2602.

For Guidance When Helping a Student:
  • Access our Blue Folder
  • Contact Supporting Our Students to help identify, intervene and mediate students who may be experiencing distress.
